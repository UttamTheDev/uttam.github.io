About Author

Uttam Kumar is a passionate learner with a background in Zoology and a growing interest in the world of web development and design. With a BSc, BEd, and a postgraduate degree in Zoology, Uttam has always been curious about how systems work—both in nature and in tech. He’s currently focused on front-end development, learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and exploring frameworks like Bootstrap. He’s also experimenting with design tools like Figma and has plans to dive into PHP for backend development.

Beyond coding, Uttam embraces a calm and creative lifestyle. He finds peace in everyday moments and enjoys spending time reflecting, exploring ideas, and learning new things. Whether it’s experimenting with design, diving into code, or sharing his thoughts, he values growth, simplicity, and meaningful connections. Through this blog, he hopes to document his journey, inspire others, and connect with like-minded learners.
